SAP Offshore Support

3i Consulting’s SAP offshore support provides a full range of technology-driven services, including business-process outsourcing; application development and maintenance and support. 3i Consulting proven methodology enables its clients to benefit from a variety of new development options, a deeper pool of skilled resources and greater cost flexibility. 3i Consulting’s strength is its well trained and experienced subject matter experts. Our team members have a rich global experience spanning across multiple regions, industries, sectors and solutions which enables us to serve better.

3i Consulting Offshore services include

»SAP Analytics – BW / BOBJ » Development (both offshore and on-site) » Enhancements, interfaces, conversions, reports & forms » Bug fixing and performance tuning » Post production support » Basis » ABAP » Security support » Functional consulting (both offshore and on-site) » Functional module support for workarounds, troubleshooting and minor customizing » Functional modules Level 1 and Level 2 support after production go-live » User manuals / documentation support
