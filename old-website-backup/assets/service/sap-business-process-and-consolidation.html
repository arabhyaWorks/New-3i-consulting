SAP BPC, a component of SAP EPM portfolio, is an application dedicated to financial processes on a unified platform. Owned by the business and designed for the end user, it is the target environment to support planning, consolidation and financial reporting. The SAP Business Planning and Consolidation, version for SAP NetWeaver application improves productivity by streamlining the bottom-up and top-down financial and operational planning process from a single application and intuitive user interface. You can plan more efficiently and improve budget cycle times by leveraging native Microsoft Excel capability.

SAP BPC

  • Supports top-down and bottom up planning leading to timely, more accurate plans that are aligned with strategic goals
  • Facilitates collaborative planning across all functions for more accurate budgets
  • Enables continuous planning and rolling forecasts to meet rapidly changing business conditions and reduce budget cycle time
  • Automates the consolidation process, meeting all financial reporting requirements with a complete audit trail for a fast, accurate close

3i’s Value Proposition:

3i has proven expertise in implementing SAP BPC for number of clients. For organizations struggling with increased headcount costs and lost revenue as a result of inefficient processes and complicated group reporting systems, 3i can improve the overall effectiveness of the management function by streamlining business processes using a combination of cutting edge software from SAP and proven consulting of 3i experts. SAP BPC can be initially implemented to address one business process such as management consolidation but quickly be extended to address other processes e.g. business modeling, planning and budgeting. This organic scalability is a unique differentiator of SAP BPC’s unified platform.
