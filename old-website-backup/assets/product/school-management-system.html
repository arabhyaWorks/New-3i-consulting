School Management System

Re-define your school eco-system with our effective School Management Software. The software has a robust working with an easy to use interface for all the stake holders. Individual Logins for Teachers, Students and Parents help in building better communication amongst them. The School Management System provides various modules for each process right from Admissions to Exams and to issuing crucial documents like the transfer certificate.

The School Management ERP module is split into various manageable sub-modules for handling specific tasks. The ERP module takes on extensive tasks like admission management, teacher management, student/parent management, fee management, exam management and attendance management. And to add on, we have included various other convenient features like the Biometrics and Bus tracking system.Every detail is stored on a robust cloud making it easy for online accessibility and availability, thus making our ERP module work faster. The system is absolutely scalable with multiple users accessing the system at the same time.The School Management System module can be customized easily to the core requirement of the school. It is always easy to add or delete modules according to the requirement. It just a matter of plug in and play!
